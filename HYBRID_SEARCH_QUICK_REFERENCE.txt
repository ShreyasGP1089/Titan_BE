SCORING FORMULA
  final_score = (semantic × 0.6) + (keyword × 0.4)

KEYWORD SCORES
  Product name match:    1.0
  Category match:        0.6
  Description match:     0.3
  Exact match bonus:    +0.2

EXAMPLE
  Product: "Golf Iron Set"
  Keyword: "clubs"
  
  - "iron" in name → keyword = 1.0
  - Semantic similarity → semantic = 0.85
  - Final = (0.85 × 0.6) + (1.0 × 0.4) = 0.91

VALIDATION KEYWORDS
  Golf Clubs: club, iron, driver, putter, wood, wedge
  Golf Balls: ball, balls
  Golf Bag: bag, trolley, cart
  Golf Gloves: glove, gloves
