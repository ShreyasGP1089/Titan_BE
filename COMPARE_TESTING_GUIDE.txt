SCORING REFERENCE
-----------------

100 points: Exact normalized name match
90 points:  100% query token coverage
80 points:  90%+ coverage
70 points:  80%+ coverage
60 points:  70%+ coverage
50 points:  60%+ coverage
40 points:  50%+ coverage (minimum threshold)

Penalties/Bonuses:
-10: Numeric mismatch (descriptive numbers)
+5:  Model identifiers present and matched
0:   Missing mandatory model identifier (discard)
