SOLUTION
================================================================================

Scoring Formula:
  final_score = (semantic_score × 0.6) + (keyword_score × 0.4)

Keyword Scoring:
  - Product name match: 1.0 (highest priority)
  - Category match: 0.6
  - Description match: 0.3
  - Exact match boost: +0.2

Example:
  Product: "Golf Iron Set"
  Keyword: "clubs"
  
  - "iron" in name → keyword_score = 1.0
  - Semantic similarity → semantic_score = 0.85
  - Final score = (0.85 × 0.6) + (1.0 × 0.4) = 0.91
